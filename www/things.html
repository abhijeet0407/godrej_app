Things to do

Tip 1

Mi Marathi!
Even if you understand 23 languages, a true Punekar responds in Marathi

Tips 2

Take glory in the past!
Whether it is the battles of Maratha Empire, Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace or trek to Sinhagad Fort

Tips 3

Snack like a Punekar!
Lose yourself at Kayani Bakery, German Bakery; can’t miss Misal pav, Vada pav and Joshi wade

Tips 4

Learn the abbreviations!
Koregaon Park? Mahatma Gandhi Road? Fergusson College? Nah! It’s KP, MG and FC where you can shop till you drop!

Tips 5

Work Hard, Party Harder!
Hit the dance floor @ Area 51, High Spirits or 1000 Oaks! You prefer the outdoors? Treks, caves, beaches and lakes, Pune has got it all!
